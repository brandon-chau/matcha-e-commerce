What is Matcha?


Made from shade-grown tea leaves, primarily from the Camellia sinensis plant, Matcha is a type of powdered green tea that has its origins in Japan. It is known for its vibrant green color, unique flavor, and a wide range of health benefits which has gained popularity worldwide in recent years and are commonly found in cafes, stores, and traditional tea ceremonies.
